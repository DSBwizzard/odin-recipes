Tomato Bisque

Servings: 6

Calories: 680

Highlights

A creamy, delicious tomato puree soup.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup chopped basil
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 yellow onion
  • 4 carrots
  • 4tsp garlic
  • 6 celery ribs
  • 6tbsp butter
  • 8 cups chicken broth
  • 8oz tomato paste
  • 12oz parmesan cheese
  • 60oz canned fire-roasted tomatoes
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Add canned tomatoes, vegetables, broth, basil, and seasonings to crock pot.
  2. Cook on low for 6-8 hours, or high for 2-3 hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. In two batches, carefully place soup in blender and puree until smooth, then return to crock pot.
  4. Make a roux:
    1. Melt butter in a suacepan over medium heat.
    2. Whisk in flour to create a paste.
    3. Slowly whisk in flour and CREAM (Edit recipe to include cream to make roux GOOD) until smooth.
  5. Stir roux and cheese into soup. Continue to cook for 30-40 minutes.
  6. Stir before serving, and serve with a slice of sourdough.
