Cheeseburger

Description

This is a copycat, make-it-at-home recipe of Smashburger's formula for a delicious cheeseburger. They’re minced beef and salt, smashed flat over a searingly hot flat-grill until you get a crispy, craggy-edged, asymmetric, cooked-through patty that’s juicy, caramelised, and beefy.

You can mix it up with the toppings, but try to keep it simple. This is a burger that's all about the beef. Consider sliced onions, dill pickles, some shredded lettuce, and a mayo-based condiment.
