Jalapeno Popper Dip

Description

This jalapeno popper dip is rich and creamy with all the yummy flavors of bacon-wrapped jalapeno poppers. Your vegetarian friends can enjoy too - the bacon is actually fake! Served warm with crackers or chips, this spicy dip is sure to be a crowd-pleaser on game day or anytime you need a delicious appetizer!

You can modify the heat level as much as you'd like by adding or removing jalapenos. If you want a milder dip, skip the minced jalapeno all-together and just include the can of diced green chilis.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 can (4 ounces) diced green chilis
  • 1 jalapeno pepper deseeded, deveined and minced
  • 4 tablespoons imitation bacon bits
  • 1 cup crushed round butter crackers, or 1 cup panko bread crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • Crackers, vegetables, or chips for serving

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. In a large bowl, mix mayo, cream cheese, shredded cheese, green chiles, imitation bacon bits, and jalapeno.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine melted butter and cracker crumbs.
  4. Transfer jalapeno popper dip into a baking dish, spread out evenly. Top with the crumb-butter mixture.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes - serve warm with crackers, tortilla chips or dippable of choice.
